Day 5 — Kyoto Old Town

Thursday, 14 May 2026  ·  History & Culture

Suggested Spots
  • Yasaka Shrine — free entry, beautiful traditional architecture in Gion
  • Nishiki Market — narrow covered market, "Kyoto's Kitchen"

Getting Around

Most of the old town is walkable. Use the ICOCA card for bus rides between areas.
