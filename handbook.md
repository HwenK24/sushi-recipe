### Setting Up ICOCA Card (Transit Card)
Top up **¥5,000** (~RM 125) per person. Used for all trains and buses throughout the trip.

**How to add ICOCA on iPhone:**
1. Open the **Wallet** app
2. Tap the **+** button (top right)
3. Select **Transit Card**
4. Search for **ICOCA** and follow the prompts
5. Top up using Wise or another card already in Wallet

## Day 2 · May 11 (Mon) — Osaka Free Day

Free day to explore Osaka at your own pace. Use your ICOCA card for all trains.

**Suggested areas:**
- **Dotonbori** — neon lights, street food, iconic photo spots

- **Kuromon Ichiba Market** — fresh food market, 5 minutes from hotel
- **Namba Parks** — shops and rooftop garden
